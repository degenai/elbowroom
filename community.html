Good To Know

On-site questions, answered

What does it cost?
We quote it up front based on the hours and roughly how many people. Booked by the hour or by the session, with no contracts, no memberships, and no minimums to lock into.
What do you need from us?
A corner with an outlet and people who want a few minutes in the chair. We bring the chair, the supplies, and the licensed therapist. That is the whole list.
Does anyone have to undress?
No. Chair massage is fully clothed and seated, no oil, no lotion. It works in any office, lobby, or event space without a private room.
How long does each person take?
About five minutes per chair keeps a line moving through a big group. If you would rather give each person a longer session, we can set the rotation up that way instead.
