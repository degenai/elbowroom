Elbow Room — business cards

Standard US business card, 3.5in × 2in. Two faces: a brand + offer front and a write-in appointment back (the differentiator). Print at 100% scale on cardstock and turn ON “Background graphics” so the navy and gold read.

Render targets (headless Edge --print-to-pdf): ?print=front, ?print=back, ?print=sheet (10-up on US Letter, Avery 5371).
