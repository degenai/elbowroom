Good To Know

Questions, answered

Do I need a membership or package?
No. You book and pay per session, that is it. No monthly fee, no package to buy down, and nothing to cancel later.
How do I pay?
Payment is handled at your appointment: Venmo, Cash App, or card. No card on file and no online prepay, so booking a time costs you nothing up front.
What kind of massage do you do?
Clinical and therapeutic work: deep tissue, trigger point, myofascial release, and relaxation when that is what the day calls for. Good for chronic pain, arthritis, old injuries, and everyday stiffness alike. Seated chair massage is available on-site for teams and events.
Where are you located?
Woodstock, GA, working out of trusted partner locations across Cherokee and Cobb counties. See the locations page for current sites and hours.
